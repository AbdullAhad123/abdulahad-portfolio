If you’re interested in skincare, you’ve probably heard the term skin barrier thrown around. So what’s the big deal?

Your outermost layer of skin is called the stratum corneum, AKA the skin barrier.2 Under a microscope, it looks like a layer of bricks with mortar between them. 

The “bricks” are skin cells, and the “mortar” is made up of compounds like cholesterol, ceramides, and fatty acids.

BUT… the gaps between your skin cells can get damaged or leaky. 

That means irritants and toxins can get into your skin, and moisture leaks out!

Thankfully, Ultimate Glow Moisturizer is focused on skin barrier restoration.
