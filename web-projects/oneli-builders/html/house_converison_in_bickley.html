Our Approach

In tackling this extensive renovation project, we prioritized the client's vision and needs. Our team worked collaboratively with architects and designers, leveraging advanced construction techniques and high-quality materials to achieve the desired outcomes.

Planning & Design

Our approach to this project began with a series of in-depth consultations to fully understand the client’s requirements, preferences, and vision for their home. We took the time to discuss every detail, ensuring that all aspects of the project were aligned with their expectations. This collaborative effort was essential in developing a set of comprehensive architectural plans that precisely reflected the client’s desires.

Initial Consultation

We met with the client multiple times to gather detailed information about their needs and preferences. This included discussions about the desired layout, design aesthetics, functionality, and specific features they wanted to incorporate.

3D Renderings and Visualization

To help the client visualize the final outcome, we created detailed 3D renderings of the proposed designs. These visual tools allowed the client to see a realistic representation of the changes and make informed decisions about the design elements.

Permits and Regulations

With the design finalized, we moved on to securing all necessary permits. Our team navigated the regulatory landscape to ensure that all aspects of the project were compliant with local building codes and regulations. This step was crucial in preventing any legal or procedural delays during construction.

Final Design Approval

Before commencing construction, we conducted a final review with the client to ensure every detail was perfect. We made any necessary adjustments based on their feedback, ensuring complete satisfaction with the design plan.

Construction & Execution

The construction phase was carried out with meticulous attention to detail, ensuring that each aspect of the project was executed to the highest standards.

Side Extension

We started with the side extension, creating additional living space that seamlessly integrated with the existing structure. This involved careful planning to ensure that the new addition matched the architectural style and aesthetic of the original building. The result was a functional and visually pleasing expansion that enhanced the overall value and usability of the home.

Loft Conversion

The loft conversion was one of the most significant aspects of the project. We transformed the loft area with a hip to gable conversion, effectively adding a new bedroom and bathroom. This conversion not only maximized the available space but also provided the family with much-needed additional living areas. The new spaces were designed with comfort and practicality in mind, featuring high-quality finishes and thoughtful layouts.

Kitchen Installation

The installation of a modern, state-of-the-art kitchen was a highlight of the renovation. We installed high-end appliances and incorporated smart storage solutions to create a functional and stylish kitchen. The design focused on maximizing space and improving workflow, making the kitchen a central hub for family activities.

Full House Refurbishment

The final step was a comprehensive refurbishment of the entire house. We updated the flooring, lighting, and decor throughout, ensuring a cohesive and modern look. Each room was given a fresh, new appearance that reflected the client's personal style. The updated decor, combined with the new flooring and lighting, created a harmonious and inviting atmosphere throughout the home.
