Meet the Visionaries Behind Our Success

Get acquainted with the brilliant minds driving our achievements. Our team is comprised of passionate experts dedicated to realizing your dreams. With skill, innovation, and a shared commitment to excellence, we make the extraordinary possible.

Michael Rieger

DIRECTOR

With 27 years of industry expertise, Michael, a Business degree graduate from UNYP, managed underground gas storage projects for RWE before leading his family firm in Czechia. In 2015, he founded Professional Carpentry Services in London, personally overseeing projects since.

Alzbeta Rieger

DESIGNER

Alzbeta is a graduate in Fashion and Textile Design from the Technical University of Liberec, has over a decade of experience in Interior Design and Building Space Solutions. With expertise in light, colors, and materials, she crafts truly unique, innovative and harmonized spaces.
