Full Job Description

Fulcrum Pvt. Ltd. is urgently hiring for "Sr Generator Operator" for one of their reputed client located Karachi.

Position:Sr. Generator Operator

Category:Information Technology

Location:Luckyone Mall

Job type:Luckyone Mall

Education:BSCS/BSE

Experience:1 to 2 Years

Age:18 to 30

Timings:10 am to 7 pm

Salary:50k to 55k a month

Additional Requirments:None

Main Responsibilities

  • To Have a complete round of the plant. Check all temperature, pressures regarding engines and auxiliaries, level of cooling towers, underground and overhead tanks.
  • To Control generator output to match the phase, frequency, or voltage of electricity supplied to panels
  • Check all running and stand by machinery for correct operation. Check tools box for complete tools as per list.
  • Also exclusively check Generators, Cable trays, Control room panel, breakers, Circulation pumps motors, Cooling towers, Water transfer pumps, Basement, Lube oil tanks, Sui gas pressure, Air filter, Batteries, Diesel Tanks, Diesel
  • To maintain log books, registers and other reports.
  • To check the values of PH and TDS after every hour and log it into the log book & maintain these values with in their required limits.
  • To maintain all parameters regarding whole system.
  • Put down all schedule work carried out during the watch and also note any abnormality found in the log book before handing over the charge. Also inform verbally to the incoming shift in charge use of personal computers and related software applications.
  • Clean engine mat filter and panel filter for both gas engine.
  • Carry out timely cleaning of filter system and ventilation system
