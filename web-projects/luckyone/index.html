How it works

Apply Process

Search a Job

Browse through our diverse job listings to find the perfect match for your skills and interests. Use filters to narrow down your search by department, location, and job type. Start your journey with us today!

Submit your Application

Once you’ve found the right job, click on the apply button and fill out the application form. Make sure to upload your updated CV and provide any necessary information to complete your submission.

Recruiters Approval

After you submit your application, our recruitment team will review your qualifications and experience. If your profile matches our requirements, you’ll be contacted for further steps, including an interview.

Get your Job

If you successfully pass the interview process, you will receive an official job offer. Once accepted, you’ll join our team at LuckyOne Mall, where your exciting new career awaits. Welcome aboard!
